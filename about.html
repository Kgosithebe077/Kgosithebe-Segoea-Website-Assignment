About Us

Recently established in Gaborone, Sahara Blend has cultivated exceptional coffee in Botswana's fertile highlands. Our family-owned farms use sustainable practices to bring you the richest Arabica and Robusta beans, carefully roasted to perfection.

We partner directly with local farmers, ensuring fair wages and preserving traditional cultivation methods. Every cup of Sahara Blend coffee carries the essence of African sunshine and generations of expertise.
