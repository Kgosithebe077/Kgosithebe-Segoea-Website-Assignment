The Home Of Africa's Richest Coffee Beans

Nestled in the fertile soils of northern Botswana, our coffee plantations cultivate three exceptional varieties that define the Sahara Blend experience. Our Arabica Supreme delivers smooth sophistication, while our robust Robusta Gold provides bold intensity. Completing our premium collection is the distinctive Sankara blend, a unique heritage variety that captures the essence of Botswana's rich agricultural traditions. Each bean is carefully hand-selected and processed to ensure the highest quality reaches your cup.
